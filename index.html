Risk Analysis & Mitigation
⚡ Founder concentration risk
Medium · Managed
Two projects with two founders whose skills are clearly divided — Phuc leads brand + guest experience, Tai leads sales + systems. Risk reduces over time as each project builds its own operational leadership.
✓ Mitigation: Hao Duong supports Phuc in operations management from Q3 2026. Wayflo developer hired in 2027. Both Phuc and Tai committed through 2035 — successors appointed by function, not a single GM hire.
🌊 Tourism seasonality
Medium
Vietnam inbound tourism peaks Oct–Apr and dips May–Sep. High variance in monthly revenue creates cash flow pressure in low season.
✓ Mitigation: Wayflo SaaS revenue is non-seasonal — provides a recurring counter-balance to tour seasonality. Low-season South Vietnam products (Mekong, Con Dao) fill the Ant Adventures gap.
📡 B2B concentration risk
High · Year 1
Year 1, The Ant Adventures is reliant on 3 core B2B partners (Charu, Make Your Asia, Adi). Loss of one partner could impact 30–40% of bookings.
✓ Mitigation: No single agent to exceed 35% of revenue. Website launch (July 2026) activates direct booking channel. US Travel Fair 2027 diversifies to international agents.
⚡ Wayflo adoption risk
Medium
Vietnam tourism operators are often not tech-savvy buyers. Convincing them to pay for CRM software requires significant education and proof.
✓ Mitigation: The Ant Adventures is the living case study — we use Wayflo ourselves. Real testimonials, not sales pitch. Start with operators in our existing network (Make Your Asia, Adi, etc.).
💱 Currency & macro risk
Low
USD-denominated tourism revenue with VND costs creates some exposure. Global economic downturns could reduce luxury travel spend.
✓ Mitigation: Wayflo is a VND-based SaaS business providing natural currency diversification. UHNW segment is the most resilient to economic cycles globally.
